Legendary Story

The Legacy of My Hero Academia

Kohei Horikoshi's masterpiece redefined modern shonen storytelling with emotional depth, unforgettable characters, and epic battles.

From the first chapter in 2014 to its monumental conclusion, My Hero Academia became a global phenomenon. It explored what it truly means to be a hero—not just through strength, but through sacrifice, compassion, and perseverance. Every arc builds upon the central theme: anyone can become someone's hero.
