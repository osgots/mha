My Hero Academia Movies

From explosive battles to emotional character arcs, experience every cinematic adventure of Deku, Bakugo, Todoroki, and the heroes of U.A. High.

The Cinematic Journey

The My Hero Academia films expand the anime universe with original stories, global stakes, and stunning animation. Each movie delivers larger-than-life action while deepening the bonds between heroes, classmates, and mentors.

Movie Collection

2018

Two Heroes

Deku and All Might visit I-Island, where a celebration of Quirk innovation turns into a dangerous hostage crisis. A perfect blend of action, science, and legacy.

  • First MHA theatrical film
  • Focus on All Might's past
  • Introduces Melissa Shield
2019

Heroes Rising

Class 1-A faces one of its greatest challenges when a powerful villain attacks Nabu Island. Teamwork, sacrifice, and unforgettable battles define this fan-favorite film.

  • Entire Class 1-A shines
  • Epic Deku & Bakugo finale
  • High emotional stakes
2021

World Heroes' Mission

Heroes from around the globe unite to stop Humarise, a radical organization threatening humanity. Deku, Bakugo, and Todoroki take center stage in this worldwide adventure.

  • Global hero mission
  • Introduces Rody Soul
  • International stakes
2024

You're Next

A mysterious figure inspired by All Might emerges, challenging the meaning of heroism itself. A new era, new threats, and a powerful test for the next generation.

  • New original villain
  • Symbol of Peace legacy
  • Next-generation hero themes
